# TSC Event + Academy Strategy

## Reference model

The reference model discussed for TSC is the Travis Scott x Fragment x Jordan Brand activation known as **Hijacked: A Tokyo Bay Takeover**.

Founder-provided notes describe the activation as a one-night Tokyo Bay/Yokohama Hammerhead event tied to the Travis Scott x Fragment Air Jordan 1 Low launch, with cars, sneakers, music, live art, fashion, and sim racing in one world.

Public-facing Atlas/TSC materials should verify details before making factual marketing claims. Internally, this model is used as inspiration for structure, not as something to copy.

## TSC interpretation

TSC is not trying to duplicate a celebrity activation. TSC is building a New Mexico-rooted version with its own mission:

> Culture brings people in. Skill-building gives them a path forward.

## Flagship concept: TSC Culture Garage

TSC Culture Garage is a cultural activation around:

- AUTO
- ART
- MUSIC
- FASHION
- TECH
- EDUCATION

It should feel like a premium garage, streetwear studio, music room, live art lab, sim racing pit, fabrication class, and founder-led community platform in one controlled environment.

## Culture Garage 001: small proof event

Start private and controlled. Suggested scale: 20 to 40 people.

## Academy model

The academy side is inspired by youth design, fabrication, fashion, and STEM programs such as the Cactus Jack / design education lane discussed with the founder.

TSC Academy should become a culture-to-career pipeline connecting:

- automotive engineering
- fabrication
- fashion and product design
- audio/electronics
- sim racing and vehicle dynamics
- live art
- music and production
- content creation
- entrepreneurship
- AI workflow through Atlas

## Education angle

The pitch is not: "We want to throw an event."

The pitch is:

> We are building a culture-to-career pipeline where cars, art, fashion, music, sim racing, and AI help young builders see a path into design, engineering, business, and community leadership.

## UNM / FSAE bridge

The FSAE lane is important because it turns automotive culture into real engineering credibility.

TSC should approach this humbly:

1. student-first learning
2. tour and volunteer path
3. content/storytelling support
4. community-facing education later
5. no logo use, merch use, or official partnership language without permission

## What makes TSC different

A major celebrity activation says: "Look how exclusive this world is."

TSC should say:

> Come learn how to build your way into this world.
