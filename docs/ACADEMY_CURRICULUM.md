# TSC Academy Curriculum

## Purpose

TSC Academy turns culture into skill. The academy should help students, builders, artists, and community members see how automotive culture, fashion, music, art, and technology connect to real career and business pathways.

## Track 1: Automotive + Fabrication

Topics:

- shop safety
- tools and measurement
- vehicle systems basics
- detailing and glass work
- fabrication vocabulary
- fasteners, brackets, panels, and fixtures
- intro to motorsport thinking
- FSAE as a bridge to engineering

Outcome: learners understand how car culture connects to engineering, fabrication, and disciplined build work.

## Track 2: Fashion + Product Design

Topics:

- brand identity
- hats, shirts, workwear, and product blanks
- embroidery and print methods
- fit, material, and sample quality
- drop planning
- product photography
- pricing basics
- streetwear storytelling

Outcome: learners understand how a clothing idea becomes a physical product and brand story.

## Track 3: Audio + Electronics

Topics:

- car audio signal path
- wiring basics
- safety and fusing
- enclosure design concepts
- speaker/subwoofer basics
- tuning and measurement
- sponsor-ready documentation
- build reactions and demos

Outcome: learners understand car audio as engineering, craft, and storytelling.

## Track 4: Sim Racing + Vehicle Dynamics

Topics:

- racing line
- braking points
- throttle control
- lap-time comparison
- telemetry basics
- suspension vocabulary
- tire grip and weight transfer
- FSAE and motorsport analysis

Outcome: learners use sim racing as a gateway into vehicle dynamics and race engineering.

## Track 5: CAD + Digital Fabrication

Topics:

- sketches and dimensions
- CAD basics
- AutoCAD/Fusion/SolidWorks concepts
- 3D printing
- CNC and laser cutting concepts
- fixtures and templates
- from digital design to physical part

Outcome: learners connect ideas, measurements, and fabrication.

## Track 6: Live Art + Visual Identity

Topics:

- legal walls and safe art spaces
- mural panels
- graffiti history and respect
- paint systems
- lettering and composition
- vehicle/art/fashion crossover
- brand worldbuilding

Outcome: learners use visual art to tell a story and create a physical brand environment.

## Track 7: Music + Production

Topics:

- DJ basics
- beat structure
- sound selection
- event audio flow
- local artist showcases
- music as atmosphere
- music recap planning

Outcome: learners understand how sound shapes an event and brand world.

## Track 8: Content + Sponsorship

Topics:

- shot lists
- reels
- founder videos
- sponsor deliverables
- recap decks
- captions
- proof-of-work packages
- media etiquette

Outcome: learners know how to document work in a way sponsors, mentors, and community partners can understand.

## Track 9: Atlas AI Workflow

Topics:

- task tracking
- event checklists
- grant tracking
- contact pipeline
- sponsor follow-ups
- research agents
- content planning
- privacy rules
- offline/survival mode basics

Outcome: learners understand how AI can support organization, learning, business, and safety without replacing human judgment.

## Academy principle

Do not make TSC Academy feel like school wearing a fake streetwear hoodie. Make it hands-on, useful, respectful, and rooted in real building.
